What is the meaning of VolleyBall?

a game in which two teams use their hands to hit a large ball from one side of a high net to the other, without allowing the ball to touch the ground.

When was the VolleyBall discovered?

William G. Morgan is the inventor and he invented VolleyBall in February 9, 1895.

Who had the idea for making the VolleyBall?

William G. Morgan had the idea so he invented it.
